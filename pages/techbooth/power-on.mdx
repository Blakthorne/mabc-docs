# Power On & Configure Equipment


## Mixer

1. Turn on with the rocker switch on the rear right
2. In *Scenes* on the right side of the board, click *View*
3. Use left scroll knob under the screen to find the scene called *Default Service*
4. Press knob to select the scene
5. Move the *Main* fader to *Unity* (0)


## Amp

Turn on with push button on the front right


## Computer

Turn on with small, round, inset button on the rear right


## Mother's Room TV

Turn on with remote (red light on T.V. turns on)


## Camera

1. Connect power cable on right side
2. Connect mini HDMI cable on left side
3. Power on with rectangular button on left side
4. Open lens cover with switch on right side
